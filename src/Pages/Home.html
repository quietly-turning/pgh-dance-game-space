PGH Dance Game Space

About

The PGH Dance Game Space is a Pittsburgh-based community center for dance gamers and dance-game-adjacent folk.

We get together twice a week to share freeplay dance games, board games, food, and one another's company.

The primary goal of The Space is:
to provide a physical place where each person can socialize, share a meal, and express themselves in a safe and supportive environment.

Secondary goals include:

  1. providing the local Pittsburgh community a place to play rhythm games
  2. providing the broader global dance game community a permanent location equipped to host events
