PGH Dance Game Space

About

The PGH Dance Game Space is a Pittsburgh-based community center for dance gamers and dance-game-adjacent folk.

We get together twice a week to share freeplay dance games, board games, food, and and one another's company.

The primary goal of The Space is to provide a gathering place where the local dance game community can meet meet and engage, such that each person can socialize, have a meal, and express themselves in a safe and supportive environment.

The secondary goals of The Space include providing the local Pittsburgh dance game community with a place to play rhythm games, and providing the broader global dance game community a permanent location equipped to host events.
