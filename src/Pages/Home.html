PGH Dance Game Space

The PGH Dance Game Space is a community center for dance gamers and dance-game-adjacent-folk.

We get together twice a week to share freeplay dance games, board games, food, and and one another's company.

The primary goal of The Space is to be a gathering place where the local Pittsburgh dance game community can meet meet and engage — to ensure each person can socialize, have a meal, and express themselves to the extent they are comfortable — in a safe and supportive environment.

The secondary goals of The Space include providing the local Pittsburgh dance game community with a place to play rhythm games, and providing the broader global dance game community a place to host events.
